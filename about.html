About Us

Learn about our mission, values, and the work we do for cow protection in Goa.

Who We Are

Gomantak Gausevak Mahasang

Gomantak Mahasangh is the biggest shelter for stray cattle's in Goa. The purpose of Mahasangh is to care for stray, abandoned as well as all other Desi Cows (Gomata) & its offsprings (Govansh).

Our Goshala is a sanctuary for cows, providing a safe and nurturing environment for them to live out their lives in peace and dignity. At our Goshala, we believe that cows are not just mere animals, but divine creatures that deserve our love and respect.

We are committed to the protection and preservation of cows, and strive to promote their welfare in every way possible. Among the cows that do produce milk, priority is always given to their calves to ensure their healthy growth.

What We Provide

Our Services

Food & Nutrition

We provide fresh fodder, hay, grains, and nutritional supplements daily. Our cows receive a balanced diet tailored to their health needs.

Shelter & Care

Spacious, clean shelters with proper ventilation and bedding. Each cow has adequate space to move freely and rest comfortably.

Medical Care

Regular veterinary checkups, vaccinations, and treatment for injuries. We have a dedicated medical facility with trained staff.
